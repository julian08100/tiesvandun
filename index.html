About

Keeping peak-time techno alive.

I am Ties van Dun, a 20-year-old DJ and producer dedicated to keeping the peak-time techno sound alive. I blend modern driving techno with the raw, acid-infused energy of the early days — constructing mixes that emphasise tension, flow, and high-energy moments.

My goal is to bring new life to peak-time and acid-driven sounds and show that techno can be both powerful and emotional.

Active since age 18. Performed at an Indira Paganotto showcase at Amsterdam Dance Event 2023. Won the Klaudia Gawlas competition in Germany — earning a mainstage opening slot from over 200 submissions.
